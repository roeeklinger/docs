BitKit is an AI-powered web data platform that turns natural language questions into structured insights. Ask anything about brands, competitors, or trends across social media and the open web — BitKit fetches the data, analyzes it, and delivers results through a dashboard, REST API, or SDK of your choice.

## How BitKit works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your query">
    Type a question in plain English — for example, "How many people mentioned our brand on X last week?"
  </Step>
  <Step title="BitKit fetches the data">
    BitKit executes your query against the open web, pulling results from the sources you care about: X, Reddit, LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze and visualize">
    Results come back as structured data with charts, sentiment breakdowns, and summaries — ready to explore in the dashboard or export via API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Integrate and automate">
    Schedule recurring jobs, push results to S3-compatible storage, or stream updates to your stack via webhooks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
